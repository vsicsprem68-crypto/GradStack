About GradStack

Who We Are

GradStack is a student-centric learning platform designed to simplify and strengthen college-level education. Our platform brings essential academic resources together in one place, helping students study smarter, stay organized, and track their progress effectively.

We understand the challenges students face while searching for reliable notes, previous year question papers, and structured practice material. GradStack solves this problem by offering a clean, focused, and easy-to-use learning environment.

What We Offer

GradStack provides a comprehensive set of tools and resources, including:

  • Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs)
  • Well-structured study notes and modules (PDF format)
  • Practice sets with solutions
  • Smart progress tracking system
  • Subject-wise, semester-wise, and topic-wise content organization

All resources are carefully structured to support systematic learning and efficient exam preparation.

Smart Progress Tracking

One of GradStack’s key features is its smart progress tracking system. Each user starts at 0%, and progress is updated automatically as courses and topics are completed.

  • Monitor completed topics
  • Identify pending areas
  • Know exactly what to study next

By providing clarity and direction, GradStack ensures focused and goal-oriented learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make higher education more accessible, organized, and effective for college students. We aim to reduce confusion, save time, and help students achieve better academic outcomes through structured learning resources.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a trusted and scalable learning platform that supports thousands of students in their academic journey and contributes to improved educational standards.
